3 mistakes that cost exporters money

Most export delays come from small paperwork errors. Here are the 3 most common — and how ProformaFlow handles them automatically:

1. Wrong Incoterms. DAP, DDP, FOB — using the wrong one can shift thousands of dollars of cost to you. ProformaFlow validates Incoterms against carrier requirements.

2. Missing EORI number. EU-bound shipments without EORI are returned. Our generator flags EU destinations and prompts for the number.

3. Currency mismatch. Invoice currency that doesn't match the commercial invoice triggers customs queries. We auto-detect this.
