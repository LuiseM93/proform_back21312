One last tip before we go quiet

This is our final email in the sequence. Here is the single most valuable piece of advice we can give:

Always print your commercial invoice on company letterhead. Not because customs requires it — they do not. But because it signals to the broker reviewing your shipment that this is a legitimate business, not a one-off personal shipment. Brokers process thousands of packages daily. Anything that makes yours look professional reduces scrutiny.
