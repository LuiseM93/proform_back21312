Your questions, answered

Q: Do customs authorities actually accept ProformaFlow PDFs?
Yes. Our documents follow the WTO and ICC model forms. They include all required fields: HS codes, Incoterms, EORI, country of origin, shipper/exporter/consignee details, and value breakdown.

Q: Can I add my company logo?
Yes — the Business plan ($149/mo) includes logo upload. Your logo appears on every generated PDF.

Q: How do the PDFs handle multiple line items?
Each line item gets its own row with HS code, country of origin, quantity, unit value, and total. There is no practical limit.

Q: Is my data stored?
No. All document generation happens in your browser. We do not store any of your shipment data.
