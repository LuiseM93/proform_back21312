Carrier-ready PDFs — what carriers actually check

When you send a commercial invoice to FedEx, UPS, or DHL, their system scans it before your package moves. Here is what they look for:

UPS: Requires harmonized codes and detailed description of goods. Rejects vague terms like "sample" or "gift."

FedEx: Flags invoices without country of origin for each line item. Also checks shipper's tax ID.

DHL: Strict about Incoterms — DAP and DDP treated very differently for duties billing.
